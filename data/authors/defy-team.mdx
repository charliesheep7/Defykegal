The Defy Editorial Team produces science-backed guides on kegel exercises, pelvic floor health, erectile dysfunction, and male sexual wellness. Our writers have backgrounds in health journalism and biomedical communication.

Every article we publish is independently reviewed for clinical accuracy by Dr. Lu, MD — an Associate Chief Physician and Director of Health Examination with nearly 20 years of clinical experience in chronic disease prevention and integrated medicine. We cite peer-reviewed research from PubMed-indexed journals, NIH, and major academic medical centers. We do not publish AI-generated content without human clinical oversight.

Our editorial standards are documented in full on our [editorial policy](/editorial-policy) page.
