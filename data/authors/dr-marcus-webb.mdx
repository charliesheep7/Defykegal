Dr. Lu is an Associate Chief Physician, Associate Professor, and Director of the Health Examination Department with nearly 20 years of clinical experience spanning internal medicine, chronic disease management, and men's urological health. She holds a Bachelor's degree in Clinical Medicine from Shanghai Railway Medical College and a Master's degree in Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine from Guangzhou University of Chinese Medicine (2009).

She is a member of the Health Management Committee of the Guangzhou Medical Association's Health Promotion Association. Her clinical work encompasses male lower urinary tract symptoms, pelvic floor dysfunction assessment, erectile dysfunction screening, and the intersection of chronic disease with male sexual and urinary health. Since 2009, Dr. Lu has led health examination and preventive health management programs, with a long-term research focus on the prevention, screening, diagnosis, and differential diagnosis of chronic conditions — including those affecting men's pelvic and urological function.

Dr. Lu reviews Defy's health content for clinical accuracy, ensuring articles on kegel training, pelvic floor rehabilitation, ED, and urinary control reflect current evidence-based medical guidelines.
