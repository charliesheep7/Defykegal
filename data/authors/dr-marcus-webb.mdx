Dr. Marcus Webb is a board-certified urologist and Fellow of the American College of Surgeons (FACS) with over 15 years of clinical experience in male pelvic floor rehabilitation, erectile dysfunction, and urinary health. He completed his urology residency at a major academic medical center and holds certification from the American Board of Urology.

Dr. Webb specializes in the conservative management of male lower urinary tract symptoms, pelvic floor muscle dysfunction, and ED — the conditions where guided kegel training produces the most meaningful clinical improvement. He serves as medical reviewer for Defy's health content, ensuring every article reflects current urological guidelines and peer-reviewed evidence.

His reviews focus on clinical accuracy: correct characterization of anatomy and physiology, alignment with published research (particularly landmark studies in BJU International and European Urology), and safe, realistic guidance on exercise protocols for men.
